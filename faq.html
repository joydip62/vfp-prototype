Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our books, orders, and services

We offer two shipping options: Standard delivery (5-7 business days) which is free for all orders, and Express delivery (2-3 business days) for ₹99. Orders are typically processed within 24 hours of confirmation.

We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, Digital Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, etc.), and Cash on Delivery. All payments are processed securely through Razorpay.

Click on "Account" in the header and select "Sign Up". You can create an account using your email or sign up instantly with Google. Having an account helps you track orders, save addresses, and earn reward points.

We offer a 30-day return policy for physical books in original condition. E-books and audio books are non-returnable due to their digital nature. Please see our detailed Return Policy for more information.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method. For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are processed via bank transfer.
